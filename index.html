THE BOOK APP

This is the design process I took to come up with an app from ideation to hi-fi mockups. Bookworm is a book app concept that allows you to trade and buy books in your area. Simply choose some genres that interest you and you are on your way to share your love of books with those around you at the price you want.

IDEATION

My process is started by sketching up some concepts and thinking about how I would like the user flow to take place. When I am happy with the flow I start to focus on the design of the UI elements. After some trial and error I moved on to creating some wireframes.

WIREFRAMES

I used Proto.io to create the wireframes. I used my sketches from the ideation step to get a rough layout of how the app will look and how it will be used.

BRANDING

Before I begin creating logos in Adobe Illustrator I like to create some moodboards for inspiration. From there I start sketching up some quick concepts with a pad and pencil. After I have a feel for what it will look like I try to find a color palette that works well with the style I have chosen.
